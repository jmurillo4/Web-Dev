What is UIA?

The Undocumented and Immigrant Alliance is a student organization founded in 2019, with the purpose of providing undocumented students on campus with a safe space that provides community, inclusivity, and understanding. UIA works tirelessly throughout the school year to provide students with events that promote community building and education about immigrant rights. UIA is founded on the pillars of community, education, and inclusivity.

UIA operates with an executive board, consisting of various roles that shift depending on the constituencies wants and needs. These leaders are selected by UIA's constituents on a yearly basis, with each executive member's term lasting exactly one school year. The executive board is responsible for managing the organization, providing leadership, and ensuring that the organization's mission and values are upheld.
